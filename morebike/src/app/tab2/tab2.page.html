MOREBIKE

Bicycle Mobility
Analysis System

What is MOREBIKE?

It is a system with the objective of analyzing and visualizing patterns identified in the mobility of the trips made and registered in the Bike-sharing System of Mexico City, ECOBICI.


It aims to be a reliable and dynamic source of information for the analysis of mobility between different areas of the city, as well as the identification of patterns at different times of the day, on weekdays or on weekends.

Data Source

ECOBICI has been in operation since 2010 and publishes its open data monthly in its official page.

The Data Science and Software Technology Laboratory (LCDyTS) of the Centro de Investigación en Computación (CIC) has created a database with such open data and keeps it constantly updated.

MOREBIKE uses this database to create visualizations to show the trips made from 2018 to the present.

 Tools
  • MySQL: database management system used in the LCDyTS.
  • Tableau: business intelligence software for data analysis and visualization.
  • Ionic: frontend SDK used for the creation of this webpage.

What can be seen on the Tableau Dashboards?

  1. Neighborhoods with the highest number of trips each year
  2. A comparison between weekdays and weekends mobility
  3. The neighbourhoods origin or destination of trips throughout the day
  4. Neighborhood-to-Neighborhood mobility or Local mobility within a neighborhood
  5. The distribution of montly trips at different years and hours of the day
